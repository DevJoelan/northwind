About the Team

Built on racecraft.
Driven by culture.

Northwind Motorsports is an Australian-based sim racing team built on a shared passion for competitive iRacing, with a strong focus on league racing and endurance events. What started as a few mates jumping into sessions for a laugh has grown into a structured, driven team that takes pride in both performance and culture. We're here to compete properly — but we never lose that grounded, Aussie attitude that makes it all enjoyable.

At our core, Northwind Motorsports is about teamwork. Anyone can hotlap, but league racing and endurance events are a different beast altogether. That's where consistency, racecraft, and communication come into play. We put a big emphasis on driving smart, keeping it clean, and making the right calls over the course of a race — whether it's a 30-minute sprint or a multi-hour endurance grind.

Endurance racing is where we really come into our own. Driver swaps, fuel strategy, tyre management — it's all part of the challenge, and we treat it seriously. Every stint matters, and every driver plays a role. We work together behind the scenes with shared setups, practice sessions, and race prep so that when it comes time to grid up, everyone knows their job and can deliver.

We compete across a range of organised iRacing leagues and special events, always aiming to represent Northwind Motorsports with professionalism and respect. That means racing hard but fair, avoiding unnecessary risks, and showing the kind of discipline that gets results over the long run. We're not just chasing podiums — we're building a reputation as a team that's consistent, reliable, and tough to beat when it counts.

Off the track, the environment stays relaxed and welcoming — proper Aussie style. There's always a bit of banter flying around in the Discord, but when it's time to race, everyone locks in. We back each other, help each other improve, and make sure no one's left figuring things out on their own.

Northwind Motorsports isn't just about turning laps — it's about building a team where drivers can grow, compete at a high level, and enjoy the journey. Whether you're chasing championships or just keen to be part of something solid, there's a place here for drivers who are committed, respectful, and ready to put in the work.
